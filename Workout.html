Workout Plan

Basic Workout Routine

Push-ups

Do 3 sets of 10 reps

Squats

Do 3 sets of 12 reps

Plank

Hold for 30 seconds, 3 sets

Lunges

Do 3 sets of 10 reps per leg

Bulking Phase - Push, Pull, Legs Split:

Push Day:

  • Bench Press: 4 sets x 6-8 reps
  • Overhead Shoulder Press: 3 sets x 8-10 reps
  • Incline Dumbbell Press: 3 sets x 8-10 reps
  • Tricep Dips: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Lateral Raises: 3 sets x 12-15 reps
  • Skull Crushers: 3 sets x 10-12 reps

    • Pull Day:

  • Deadlift: 4 sets x 6-8 reps
  • Pull-Ups or Assisted Pull-Ups: 3 sets x 8-10 reps
  • Bent Over Rows: 3 sets x 8-10 reps
  • Face Pulls: 3 sets x 12-15 rep
  • Bicep Curls: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Hammer Curls: 3 sets x 10-12 reps

    • Legs Day:

  • Squats: 4 sets x 6-8 reps
  • Romanian Deadlifts: 3 sets x 8-10 reps
  • Leg Press: 3 sets x 8-10 reps
  • Lunges: 3 sets x 10-12 reps per leg
  • Calf Raises: 4 sets x 12-15 reps

    • Cutting Phase - Push, Pull, Legs Split:

    Push Day:

  • Incline Bench Press: 4 sets x 8-10 reps
  • Seated Shoulder Press: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Push-Ups: 3 sets x max reps
  • Tricep Pushdowns: 3 sets x 12-15 reps
  • Lateral Raises: 3 sets x 12-15 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets x 30-60 seconds

    • Pull Day:

  • Pull-Ups or Assisted Pull-Ups: 4 sets x 8-10 reps
  • Bent Over Rows: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Seated Cable Rows: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Face Pulls: 3 sets x 12-15 reps
  • Bicep Curls: 3 sets x 12-15 reps
  • Reverse Crunches: 3 sets x 15-20 reps

    • Legs Day:

  • Front Squats: 4 sets x 8-10 reps
  • Romanian Deadlifts: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Bulgarian Split Squats: 3 sets x 10-12 reps per leg
  • Leg Press: 3 sets x 12-15 reps
  • Standing Calf Raises: 4 sets x 12-15 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets x 30-60 seconds

    • Additional Body Parts Workouts:

    Bulking Phase:

  • Chest and Triceps: Bench Press, Incline Dumbbell Press, Chest Flyes, Tricep Dips, Skull Crushers.
  • Back and Biceps: Deadlift, Pull-Ups, Bent Over Rows, Face Pulls, Bicep Curls, Hammer Curls.
  • Legs and Shoulders: Squats, Leg Press, Lunges, Romanian Deadlifts, Overhead Shoulder Press, Lateral Raises.

    • Cutting Phase:

  • Chest and Triceps: Incline Bench Press, Push-Ups, Tricep Pushdowns, Chest Flyes, Plank.
  • Back and Biceps: Pull-Ups, Seated Cable Rows, Face Pulls, Bicep Curls, Reverse Crunches.
  • Legs and Shoulders: Front Squats, Bulgarian Split Squats, Romanian Deadlifts, Seated Shoulder Press, Lateral Raises, Plank.

    • Note:

    Remember to incorporate a proper warm-up before each workout and cool down afterward. Adjust the weights and repetitions based on your comfort and progress. Additionally, prioritize a balanced diet, adequate rest, and hydration to support your training efforts and achieve your bulking and cutting goals.
